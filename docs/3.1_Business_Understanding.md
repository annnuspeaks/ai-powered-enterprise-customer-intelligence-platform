# 3.1 Business Understanding

## Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to understand customer behavior, identify customers who are likely to leave, estimate customer value, and target potential buyers. These challenges can result in reduced revenue and ineffective marketing strategies.

---

## Business Objectives

The platform aims to:

- Predict customer churn.
- Segment customers based on purchasing behavior.
- Estimate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
- Predict purchase propensity.
- Provide actionable business insights through an interactive dashboard.

---

## Machine Learning Objectives

The project will develop models for:

- Customer Churn Prediction
- Customer Segmentation
- Customer Lifetime Value Prediction
- Purchase Propensity Prediction

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it:

- Generates reliable predictions.
- Provides meaningful business insights.
- Supports data-driven decision making.
- Is deployed as a live web application.

---

## Project Scope

Included:

- Data preprocessing
- Feature engineering
- Machine Learning models
- Business Intelligence dashboard
- REST APIs
- Cloud deployment

Excluded:

- Real-time streaming data
- Mobile application
- Multi-user authentication (initial version)
