# 2.1.4 Feature Definition

## Core Features

### 1. Customer Churn Prediction

Predicts whether a customer is likely to stop purchasing from the business, enabling proactive customer retention strategies.

---

### 2. Customer Segmentation

Groups customers into meaningful segments based on purchasing behavior and customer characteristics for targeted marketing campaigns.

---

### 3. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Prediction

Estimates the long-term value of each customer, helping businesses prioritize customer acquisition and retention efforts.

---

### 4. Purchase Propensity Prediction

Predicts the likelihood of a customer making a future purchase, supporting personalized recommendations and marketing campaigns.

---

## Business Intelligence Dashboard

The platform will provide an interactive dashboard displaying business KPIs, customer insights, predictive analytics, and visual reports.

Dashboard components include:

- Executive KPI Cards
- Customer Analytics
- Revenue Analytics
- Interactive Charts
- Prediction Summary

---

## Explainable AI

Provides interpretable prediction results using Explainable AI techniques, enabling users to understand why a prediction was generated.

---

## Data Management

Supports customer dataset upload, validation, preprocessing, and preparation before Machine Learning inference.

---

## Reporting

Allows users to export prediction results and analytical reports for business use.

---

## REST API

Provides RESTful APIs for communication between the backend Machine Learning services and the frontend application.

---

## Cloud Deployment

The complete platform will be deployed as a live web application using cloud services for public accessibility.
